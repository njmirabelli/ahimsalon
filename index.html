Ahimsalon blends the Buddhist Yama ‘Ahimsa’, which in essence is the concept of non-violence towards all livings beings, with the ideology of the traditional beauty salon. At Ahimsalon, all products used are vegan, cruelty-free, organic, and sustainably-sourced. Our priority is to provide a safe haven where everyone is welcome to relax in the tranquil, no-judgment environment. We maintain focus on scalp and hair health as we believe in living a simple and natural life. Ten percent of all profits are donated monthly to animal welfare and animal rights organizations. Further information about which organizations the money is going can be found on Facebook at the beginning of every month.


Ahimsalon owner and lead stylist, Nicole Mirabelli, has been styling hair for twelve years. Her favorite services to provide include haircuts and scalp treatments. Additionally, she is certified in Oway hair color, dreadlock installation and maintenance, and hair extensions. Nicole is always enrolled in new classes where she is able to learn new services and techniques. In addition to styling hair, Nicole is also a registered architect and works full-time designing animal shelters and other animal care facilities. In her personal time, she enjoys taking care of senior and special needs animals. Her dog-son Petri, a five-pound yorkie-mix that was seized from an animal hoarder in 2013, has been Nicole’s inspiration in pursuing different ways to help animals, including opening Ahimsalon. Please help her save and protect animals today by being pampered.
